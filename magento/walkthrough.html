Application Walkthrough

Sign Up and Login

  • You can sign up and create an account in the application.

  • Users are managed in the Account & Contact in Salesforce. If you configured the Salesforce integration, the Magento customer is automatically synchronized with the Account & Contact object in Salesforce.

  • User passwords are hashed with bcrypt using a per-user salt.

Create Account in Magento.

Account information in Salesforce.

My Account

  • Users can edit their profile and preferences. This information is saved in the Magento contact table in MySQL (and synchronized with the Contact object in Salesforce if you configured Salesforce integration).
  • Shipping & Billing address come from the Magento Address table in MySQL. If you configured Salesforce integration, the Magento Address table is automatically synchronized with the Account object in Salesforce.

Create Shipping & Billing in Magento.

Shipping & Billing information in Salesforce.

Products

  • Products come from the Magento Product table in MySQL. If you configured Salesforce integration, the Magento Product table is automatically synchronized with the Product2 object in Salesforce.
  • You can add a product to your wish list or share products on social networks
